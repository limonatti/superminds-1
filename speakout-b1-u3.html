📚 Question forms · планы: going to / Present Continuous / will · вежливые вопросы · phrasal verbs

📖 Грамматика юнита

1. Question forms — порядок слов
ТипСхемаПример
Yes/Noaux + подл. + глаголDo you like jazz? Are you ready?
Wh-Wh + aux + подл.Where do you live?
Subjectбез auxWho called you? What happened?
Сравни: Who did you call? (кого?) vs Who called you? (кто?) — во втором нет do
2. Планы: going to / Present Continuous / will
going to — намерение/план: I'm going to look for a new job.
Present Continuous — договорённость на время: I'm meeting Sam at six.
will — решение сейчас / прогноз: OK, I'll help you.
3. Урок D — phrasal verbs
find out (узнать) · look for (искать) · give up (сдаться)
fill in a form (заполнить) · turn up (появиться) · figure out (сообразить)
I need to find out the time. Don't give up!

🎧 Аудирование

Weekend plans

Послушай диалог (озвучивается прямо в браузере), потом ответь на вопросы. Слушай сколько угодно раз.

Zoe: What are you doing this weekend, Ben?
Ben: I'm meeting some friends on Saturday. We're going to explore the old town.
Zoe: Sounds fun! Do you know where the new museum is?
Ben: I'm not sure. Could you tell me if it's near the station?
Zoe: I'll look it up. Actually, why don't you come with us?
Ben: I'd love to! What time are you meeting?
Zoe: Around eleven. I think it'll be busy, so let's turn up early.
Ben: Great. I'll find out the ticket price and text you.
