1. Narrative tenses — рассказываем истории о вещах
ВремяЗачемПример
Past Simpleсобытия по очередиI found this camera at a market.
Past Continuousфон, процессI was walking home when I saw it.
Past Perfectещё раньше тогоSomeone had left it on a bench.
While I was tidying the attic, I found a box that my grandad had packed fifty years before. — все три времени в одной истории
2. Verb patterns — что идёт после глагола?
+ -ing: enjoy, don't mind, suggest, imagine, keep, avoid → I enjoy collecting old maps.
+ to inf: want, decide, agree, refuse, promise, hope → She decided to sell her books.
Меняют смысл: stop to buy (остановился, чтобы купить) vs stop buying (перестал покупать)

🎧 Аудирование

The story of my old suitcase

Послушай диалог (озвучивается прямо в браузере), потом ответь на вопросы. Слушай сколько угодно раз.

Dan: That's a really old suitcase! Where did you get it?
Mia: It belonged to my grandmother. She bought it in 1965, before her first trip to Italy.
Dan: Wow. Is it leather?
Mia: Yes, real leather. It's quite damaged now, but I love it. While I was cleaning our garage last spring, I found it behind some boxes. Nobody had opened it for thirty years!
Dan: Was there anything inside?
Mia: Yes! Old photos, a silver ring and a train ticket from Rome.
Dan: Amazing. Are you going to repair it?
Mia: No, I've decided to keep it exactly as it is. It reminds me of her.
