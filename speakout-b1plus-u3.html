📖 Грамматика юнита

1. Обязанности: must / have to / need to
ФормаСмыслПример
must / have toобязанYou have to wear a badge.
mustn'tзапрещеноYou mustn't share your password.
don't have to / needn'tможно не делатьYou don't have to come early.
2. Conditionals на работе
1st (реально): If I finish early, I'll call you. — если…, то будет
2nd (фантазия): If I had my own business, I'd work from the beach. — если бы…, то бы
unless = if not: Unless you hurry, you'll miss the meeting.
3. Урок D — работа мечты
I'd love to work outdoors and travel a lot. — очень хотел бы
My dream job would be running my own café. — был бы…
What I really want is to be my own boss. — What… — выделяем главное

🎧 Аудирование

The interview

Послушай диалог (озвучивается прямо в браузере), потом ответь на вопросы. Слушай сколько угодно раз.

Mr Lane: So, Emma, tell me about your experience.
Emma: I've worked as a designer for six years. In my last job I led a small team of three people.
Mr Lane: Why did you leave?
Emma: The company was great, but I had to commute for two hours every day. If I work here, I'll be able to walk to the office!
Mr Lane: Do you prefer working alone or in a team?
Emma: In a team. If I worked alone all the time, I'd miss talking to people.
Mr Lane: When can you start?
Emma: I need to give my current boss two weeks' notice, so — on the first of March.
