Unit 6 · Creators

📚 used to · сравнительная и превосходная степень · спросить/дать мнение · Present Perfect for/since

📖 Грамматика юнита

1. used to — привычки в прошлом
I used to draw every day. — раньше, сейчас нет
Вопрос/отрицание — use to: Did you use to paint? · I didn't use to like it.
2. Comparatives & superlatives
ТипСравн.Превосх.
короткие (big)biggerthe biggest
длинные (famous)more famousthe most famous
исключенияbetter / worsethe best / the worst
Extreme adjectives: not just big → huge; not just good → amazing.
3. Урок D — Present Perfect: for / since / yet / already / just
I've lived here for five years / since 2019.
Have you finished yet? — I've just finished. / I've already seen it.

🎧 Аудирование

At the gallery

Послушай диалог (озвучивается прямо в браузере), потом ответь на вопросы. Слушай сколько угодно раз.

Ella: What do you think of this painting, Nick?
Nick: Honestly? I think it's the strangest thing I've ever seen.
Ella: Really? I love it. It's more creative than the others.
Nick: Hmm. I used to hate modern art, but this one is growing on me.
Ella: Have you seen the sculpture upstairs yet?
Nick: Not yet. Is it good?
Ella: It's amazing — the best piece in the whole exhibition, in my opinion.
Nick: OK, I've just decided: let's go and see it now.

✏️ Упражнения

💭 How to… спросить и высказать мнение

What do you think of…? — как тебе…?
In my opinion… / I reckon… — по-моему
I'm not sure about that. — мягкое несогласие
You've got a point. — тут ты прав(а)
