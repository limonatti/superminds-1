📖 Грамматика юнита

1. Present Perfect Simple vs Continuous
ФормаЗачемПример
have + V3результат, сколько сделаноI've written three emails.
have been + -ingпроцесс, как долгоI've been writing emails all morning.
How long have you been learning English? — For three years. — с for/since чаще Continuous
Состояния (know, have, be) — только Simple: I've known her since school.
2. Relative clauses — who / which / where / whose
The man who lives upstairs plays drums. — о людях
That's the café where we met. — о месте
My aunt, whose house is full of cats, never travels. — чей
The habit that/which annoys me most is loud chewing. — о вещах

🎧 Аудирование

Little habits

Послушай диалог (озвучивается прямо в браузере), потом ответь на вопросы. Слушай сколько угодно раз.

Dan: You look tired. Are you okay?
Mia: I'm fine! I've been getting up at six every morning this month.
Dan: Six?! Why?
Mia: I've taken up running. I've already run about eighty kilometres.
Dan: Impressive! I've never been a morning person. My worst habit is checking my phone, which I do about a hundred times a day.
Mia: That's the habit that annoys my brother. He says I never listen because I'm always scrolling!
Dan: Have you tried leaving your phone in another room?
Mia: Yes — that's the trick that finally worked. I've been doing it for a week, and I feel much calmer.

😤 How to… сказать, что тебя раздражает (вежливо!)

It drives me crazy when people talk in the cinema. — меня сводит с ума
I can't stand it when someone is late. — терпеть не могу
It doesn't bother me at all. — меня не напрягает
To be fair, I sometimes do it too. — честно говоря
