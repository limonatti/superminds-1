📖 Грамматика юнита

1. Reported speech — пересказываем чужие слова
СказалиПересказ
"I am busy."She said she was busy.
"I will call."He said he would call.
"I have seen it."She said she had seen it.
Reporting verbs: promise (пообещал), suggest (предложил), deny (отрицал), admit (признал), refuse (отказался): He promised to make meetings shorter.
2. Passive — когда важно ЧТО, а не КТО
Millions of messages are sent every minute. — наст.
The first email was sent in 1971. — прош.

🎧 Аудирование

The mix-up

Послушай диалог (озвучивается прямо в браузере), потом ответь на вопросы. Слушай сколько угодно раз.

Mia: You'll never guess what happened. I sent a message about my boss… to my boss.
Dan: Oh no. What did it say?
Mia: That his meetings were so long that I wanted to sleep. I meant to send it to Anna!
Dan: What did he do?
Mia: He called me to his office. I was sure I'd lose my job.
Dan: And?
Mia: He laughed! He said that everyone thought his meetings were too long, and he promised to make them shorter.
Dan: So your mistake fixed the problem. Maybe I should text my boss too!
