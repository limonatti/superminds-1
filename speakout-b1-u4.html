📚 Modals: must / have to / should · артикли a/an/the · правила can/can't · спорт и достижения

📖 Грамматика юнита

1. Modals — правила и советы
МодальныйСмыслПример
have to / mustобязательноYou have to wear trainers.
mustn'tнельзяYou mustn't touch the net.
should / shouldn'tсоветYou should warm up first.
don't have toможно неYou don't have to be a member.
2. Articles — a / an / the / —
a / an — впервые, один из многих: I saw a dog.
the — конкретное / единственное: The dog was huge. The sun is bright.
— (нет артикля) — в общем смысле: I love — sport. — Dogs are friendly.
3. Урок D — Present Perfect + superlative
It's the best game I've ever played.
She's the fastest runner I've ever seen.
This is the most exciting match we've had this year.

🎧 Аудирование

After the match

Послушай диалог (озвучивается прямо в браузере), потом ответь на вопросы. Слушай сколько угодно раз.

Mia: We won! I can't believe it, Leo!
Leo: Best game I've ever played! Did you see my last goal?
Mia: Amazing. But remember, we have to thank the coach.
Leo: True. She made us train so hard this month.
Mia: You don't have to be the strongest — you have to want it most.
Leo: Exactly. So, can we celebrate now?
Mia: Of course! But you mustn't forget practice on Monday.
Leo: Ha, never. This is the happiest I've ever been after a match.

🚦 How to… сказать про правила (can / can't)

You can bring water in. — можно
You can't use your phone here. — нельзя
Are we allowed to…? — разрешено ли?
Is it OK if I…? — вежливо спросить разрешения
