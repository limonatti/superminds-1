Unit 6 · Places

📚 so / such · be & get used to · город, транспорт и любимые места

📖 Грамматика юнита

1. so / such — усиливаем впечатление
so + прилагательное: The city was so noisy that I couldn't sleep.
such (a) + (прил.) существительное: It's such a beautiful ride that tourists queue for it.
There was so much traffic and so many people!
2. Привычки к новому: be / get used to + -ing
I'm used to the noise now. — уже привык(ла)
I'm getting used to driving on the left. — привыкаю (процесс)
Не путай: I used to live in a village. — раньше жил (прошлое!)

🎧 Аудирование

A new city

Послушай диалог (озвучивается прямо в браузере), потом ответь на вопросы. Слушай сколько угодно раз.

Dan: You moved to Lisbon three months ago, right? How is it?
Mia: I love it! But it took time. At first the city was so noisy that I couldn't sleep.
Dan: And now?
Mia: Now I'm used to the noise. I've even got used to the hills — there are such steep streets here!
Dan: What about transport?
Mia: I take the old yellow tram. It's such a beautiful ride that tourists queue for it every morning.
Dan: Do you miss anything from home?
Mia: Snow! I used to go skiing every winter. But honestly — the ocean is a good exchange.
