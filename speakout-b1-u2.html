1. Narrative tenses — рассказываем историю
ВремяЗачемПример
Past Simpleсобытия по очередиI heard a noise and opened the door.
Past Continuousфон, процессIt was raining when I left.
Past Perfectещё раньшеThe film had started when we arrived.
2. Past Simple vs Present Perfect
Past Simple — точное прошлое: I saw that film yesterday.
Present Perfect — опыт / без даты: I've seen it three times. — ever, never, just, already, yet
Have you ever read it? — No, not yet.
3. Урок D — предлоги времени
at 6 o'clock · on Monday · in July / 2020 — точка / день / период
during the film · for two hours · two years ago
while I was reading, the phone rang. — while + процесс

🎧 Аудирование

A strange night

Послушай диалог (озвучивается прямо в браузере), потом ответь на вопросы. Слушай сколько угодно раз.

Amy: You look tired, Tom. Did you sleep badly?
Tom: Terribly. Something strange happened last night.
Amy: Ooh, tell me! What happened?
Tom: Well, I was reading in bed when I heard a noise downstairs.
Amy: Scary! Had you locked the door?
Tom: Yes, I had. So I went down very quietly... and it was just the cat!
Amy: Ha! Your cat has done that before, hasn't it?
Tom: Twice this week! I've never been so frightened by a cat in my life.
