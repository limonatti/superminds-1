Unit 4 · Fact or fiction?

📚 was going to · косвенные вопросы · правда, ложь и новости

📖 Грамматика юнита

1. Несбывшиеся планы: was/were going to
I was going to call you, but I forgot. — собирался, но…
We were planning to fly to Rome, but the flight was cancelled.
2. Косвенные вопросы — вежливо и без инверсии
Where is the station? → Could you tell me where the station is? — порядок слов прямой!
Is it true? → Do you know whether/if it is true?
Don't you think the story sounds strange? — отрицательный вопрос-сомнение

🎧 Аудирование

The spaghetti tree

Послушай диалог (озвучивается прямо в браузере), потом ответь на вопросы. Слушай сколько угодно раз.

Dan: I read about a famous TV hoax yesterday. In 1957, a British programme showed farmers picking spaghetti from trees!
Mia: Spaghetti trees? You're joking.
Dan: No! It was April Fool's Day. The programme said Switzerland was having a wonderful spaghetti harvest.
Mia: And people believed it?
Dan: Hundreds of viewers called the channel. They wanted to know how they could grow their own spaghetti tree.
Mia: What did the channel tell them?
Dan: To put a piece of spaghetti in a tin of tomato sauce and hope for the best.
Mia: Brilliant. I was going to say people were smarter back then — but clearly not!
