1. Present Simple vs Continuous + state verbs
ФормаКогдаПример
Present Simpleпривычки, фактыShe works in a hospital.
Present Continuousсейчас / временноShe's working from home this week.
State verbs (know, like, want, believe) — только Simple: I know him well. (не «am knowing»)
Наречия частоты перед глаголом: I usually get up at seven; she is always late. — usually/often/never
2. Verb patterns — -ing или to?
+ -ing: enjoy, don't mind, finish, keep → I enjoy meeting new people.
+ to inf: want, decide, hope, plan, would like → I want to make new friends.
like можно с обоими: I like cooking = I like to cook.
3. Урок D — модификаторы (насколько?)
a bit shy · quite friendly · really / very kind — степень
not very talkative — мягкое «не очень»
С сильными прилагательными — absolutely: absolutely right / exhausted (не «very right»)

🎧 Аудирование

Meeting at a party

Послушай диалог (озвучивается прямо в браузере), потом ответь на вопросы. Слушай сколько угодно раз.

Sam: Hi! Do you mind if I sit here?
Nina: Not at all. I'm Nina. Are you a friend of Anna's?
Sam: Yeah, we work together. I'm Sam. How do you know her?
Nina: We were at university together. She's so outgoing — she knows everyone!
Sam: True. I'm a bit shy at parties, actually.
Nina: Me too, but you seem really friendly. What do you do, Sam?
Sam: I'm a graphic designer. I usually work from home, so I don't chat much.
Nina: Well, it's really nice talking to you. Let's get a coffee sometime.

✏️ Упражнения

💬 How to… начать и закончить разговор

Do you mind if I join you? — можно к вам?
