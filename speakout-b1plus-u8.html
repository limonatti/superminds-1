📚 third conditional · should have · would (прошлые привычки) · советы и уроки жизни

📖 Грамматика юнита

1. Third conditional — как могло бы быть
If I had studied harder, I would have gone to university. — если бы тогда…, то бы…
If he hadn't gone to that dance, he would never have met her.
should have + V3 — сожаление: I should have listened to you. — надо было…
2. would — привычки в прошлом
When I was little, my grandad would tell us stories every night. — бывало, рассказывал
= used to, но только для действий (не состояний!).

🎧 Аудирование

Grandad's advice

Послушай диалог (озвучивается прямо в браузере), потом ответь на вопросы. Слушай сколько угодно раз.

Dan: My grandad turned ninety yesterday. I asked him for his best life advice.
Mia: Ooh, what did he say?
Dan: When he was young, he would work seven days a week. He said he should have rested more.
Mia: Interesting. Anything else?
Dan: Yes. He said that if he hadn't gone to a dance in 1958, he would never have met my grandmother.
Mia: So his advice is… go dancing?
Dan: Exactly! Say yes to invitations. The best things happen when you least expect them.
Mia: Your grandad is a wise man. I should have asked mine the same question while I could.
