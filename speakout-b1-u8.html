Unit 8 · Know-how

📚 can / could / be able to · active & passive · описать проблему · -ing form

📖 Грамматика юнита

1. Ability — can / could / be able to
Настоящее: I can fix it. / I'm able to fix it.
Прошлое: I could swim at five. / I wasn't able to open it.
Будущее/perfect: I'll be able to help. / I've never been able to draw.
2. Active & Passive
ActivePassive
They repair phones here.Phones are repaired here.
They fixed the bug.The bug was fixed.
Формула: be + V3. Кто делает — через by: It was made by robots.
3. Урок D — -ing form
Как подлежащее: Learning to code is fun.
После предлогов: good at fixing things · interested in coding.
После глаголов: I enjoy building apps.

🔤 Слова юнита (нажми 🔊 и повтори)

🎧 Аудирование

My laptop won't work

Послушай диалог (озвучивается прямо в браузере), потом ответь на вопросы. Слушай сколько угодно раз.

Kim: Ryan, can you help me? My laptop isn't working.
Ryan: Let me see. What's wrong with it?
Kim: The screen is frozen and it won't switch on properly.
Ryan: Have you tried charging it? The battery might be dead.
Kim: I plugged it in, but nothing happened.
Ryan: Hmm. When was it last updated?
Kim: I'm not sure. I've never been able to fix these things myself.
Ryan: No worries. Fixing laptops is easy once you know how. Let's restart it.

✏️ Упражнения

🔧 How to… описать проблему

It's not working. — не работает
There's something wrong with… — что-то не так с…
It keeps crashing. — постоянно виснет
It won't turn on. — не включается
