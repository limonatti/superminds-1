Unit 5 · Consumer

📚 цель: to / in order to / so that · сравнения · покупки и реклама

📖 Грамматика юнита

1. Зачем? — clauses of purpose
I went online to compare prices. — чтобы
She saved money so that she could buy a laptop. — чтобы (+ подлежащее)
We left early in order not to miss the sale. — чтобы НЕ
Take a card in case the shop doesn't accept cash. — на случай, если
2. Сравнения — сильнее и точнее
much / far cheaper — гораздо дешевле; slightly more expensive — чуть дороже
not nearly as good as… — совсем не так хорош, как…
The more you buy, the more you save. — чем…, тем…

🔤 Слова юнита (нажми 🔊 и повтори)

🎧 Аудирование

The refund

Послушай диалог (озвучивается прямо в браузере), потом ответь на вопросы. Слушай сколько угодно раз.

Customer: Good morning. I bought these headphones here last week, but the left side has stopped working.
Assistant: I'm sorry to hear that. Have you got the receipt?
Customer: Yes, here it is. I paid forty pounds.
Assistant: Would you like a replacement or a refund?
Customer: A refund, please. To be honest, I've seen the same model online — it's much cheaper there.
Assistant: No problem. I just need your card. By the way, we're having a sale next week — everything will be half price.
Customer: Half price? Then maybe I'll come back in order to buy a new pair!
Assistant: Ha! The more you save, the more you spend — that's what my manager always says.
