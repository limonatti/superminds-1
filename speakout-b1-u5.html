Unit 5 · News

📚 Relative clauses · reported speech · рассказать новость · прогнозы will / might / going to

📖 Грамматика юнита

1. Relative clauses — who / which / that / where / whose
The journalist who wrote it is famous. — о людях
The story which / that went viral was fake. — о вещах
That's the town where it happened. · a blogger whose posts I follow.
2. Reported speech — пересказ
СказалиПересказ
"I am tired."She said she was tired.
"I will call."He said he would call.
"I saw it."She said she had seen it.
tell + кому: He told me… · say без адресата: He said…
3. Урок D — прогнозы: will / might / going to
will — уверенный прогноз: Robots will read the news.
might — возможно: It might rain tomorrow.
going to — по признакам/плану: Prices are going to rise.

🎧 Аудирование

Have you heard?

Послушай диалог (озвучивается прямо в браузере), потом ответь на вопросы. Слушай сколько угодно раз.

Lena: Have you heard the news? It's all over the internet!
Max: No, what happened?
Lena: They said the old cinema is going to close next month.
Max: Really? Who told you that?
Lena: A journalist posted it. But honestly, it might just be a rumour.
Max: Yeah, you can't trust everything that goes viral.
Lena: True. She said she would check the source and update the post.
Max: Good. If it's reliable, it'll be very sad news for the town.

📣 How to… рассказать и отреагировать на новость

Guess what! / You'll never believe this. — начать новость
Really? That's amazing! — радостная реакция
No way! — удивление
Oh no, that's terrible. — плохая новость
